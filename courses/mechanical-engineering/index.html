Course Overview

Mechanical Engineering is the broadest engineering discipline, applying principles of physics, mathematics, and materials science to design, analyze, manufacture, and maintain mechanical systems. This comprehensive course covers everything from fundamental mechanics to advanced manufacturing and thermal systems.

üìö What You'll Learn

This course provides a complete foundation in mechanical engineering, covering:

  • Solid Mechanics: Statics, dynamics, mechanics of materials, vibrations
  • Thermal Sciences: Thermodynamics, heat transfer, fluid mechanics
  • Design & Manufacturing: Machine design, CAD/CAM, manufacturing processes
  • Materials: Material properties, selection, failure analysis
  • Systems: HVAC, power generation, automotive, robotics
