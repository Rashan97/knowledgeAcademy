Introduction to Fluid Mechanics

Fluid mechanics studies the behavior of fluids (liquids and gases) at rest and in motion. It's essential for designing piping systems, pumps, turbines, aircraft, ships, and hydraulic systems. Understanding fluid flow is critical for mechanical engineers across many industries.
