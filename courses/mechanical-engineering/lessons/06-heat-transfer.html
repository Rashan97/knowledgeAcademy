Introduction to Heat Transfer

Heat transfer is the science of thermal energy transport from regions of high temperature to low temperature. Unlike thermodynamics which deals with equilibrium states and energy quantities, heat transfer focuses on the rates and mechanisms. This is essential for thermal design of engines, electronics cooling, HVAC systems, and heat exchangers.

Modes of Heat Transfer

Overview of Three Modes

Three Mechanisms: 1. Conduction: Heat transfer through solid or stationary fluid Molecular vibration and electron movement No bulk motion of material Dominant in solids Example: Heat flow through wall, metal rod 2.
