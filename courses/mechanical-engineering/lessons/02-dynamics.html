Introduction to Dynamics

Dynamics studies bodies in motion and the forces causing that motion. While statics dealt with equilibrium, dynamics analyzes accelerating systems using Newton's laws. This is fundamental to understanding vehicle motion, robotics, vibrations, and machinery.
