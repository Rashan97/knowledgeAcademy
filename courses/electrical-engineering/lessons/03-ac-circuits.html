Introduction to AC Circuits

Alternating Current (AC) is the backbone of power distribution and most electronic systems. Unlike DC which flows in one constant direction, AC periodically reverses direction following a sinusoidal pattern. Understanding AC circuits is essential for power systems, signal processing, communications, and modern electronics.
